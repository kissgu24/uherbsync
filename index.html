Core Features

Three features. Every problem solved.

01
📊
Inventory Tracking

Enter each supplement's specs and daily dosage. uHerbSync auto-calculates days remaining and alerts you when it's time to reorder — color-coded by urgency. Never run out again.

02
🎯
Tax Quota Management

Taiwan's 6-order / NT$2,000 per-order customs limit is automatically tracked per account. Select items to reorder and the app instantly flags if you're over the limit — with split-order suggestions.

03
🤖
AI Consultation Export

One tap exports your full supplement list — brand, dose, timing — in a format ready to paste into ChatGPT or Claude. Get instant AI analysis on stack completeness and recommendations.

How It Works

Four steps to a smarter reorder

1
Track Inventory

Open the app daily to see all items sorted by urgency. Push notifications remind you before you run out.

2
Build Reorder List

Check items to reorder. The app calculates the total and warns you if it exceeds your tax exemption limit.

3
Shop on iHerb

Tap each item to open iHerb in your browser. Add to cart and checkout — all within your quota.

4
Confirm Purchase

Return to the app, confirm what you bought, and inventory updates automatically. Done.
