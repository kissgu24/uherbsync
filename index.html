Our Principles

Built on transparency and privacy

We believe a tool that manages your health information should earn your trust, not assume it.

  • Free forever, zero ads — uHerbSync is free to download with no advertisements and no in-app purchases.

  • Your data stays on your device — All information is stored locally. We never upload your supplement data to any server or cloud service.

  • No account required — You do not need to create a profile, sign in, or share any personal information to use this app.

  • Honest affiliate disclosure — When affiliate links are present, they are clearly disclosed. Commissions never influence which products we surface or recommend.

About This Project

Why uHerbSync exists

uHerbSync was created by a long-time iHerb user who was frustrated by spreadsheets, forgotten reorder dates, and unexpectedly hitting the duty-free limit mid-cart-checkout. The goal was simple: one clean app that handles the logistics so you can focus on the health side — and turn your supplement routine into a structured, manageable system.

This is a solo indie project. Feedback, bug reports, and feature requests are genuinely welcome. If this tool saves you even one missed reorder, it has done its job.
