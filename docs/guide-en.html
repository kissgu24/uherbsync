Step 01
Add Your First Supplement

Tap the "＋" button at the top-right of the home screen.

Enter a supplement category name (e.g. NMN, Probiotics, Omega-3).

Your first inventory category will be created instantly.

Category names can be edited anytime.

Step 02
Manage Categories & Sub-items

Tap any category on the home screen to open its management panel:

  • Edit category name
  • Add / edit sub-items
  • Adjust remaining quantity and daily dosage
  • Delete category

Changes are saved automatically.

Step 03
Enable Daily Auto-Deduction

Toggle "Active" on any sub-item to enable daily auto-deduction.

The system will reduce inventory automatically each day.

Best for supplements taken on a fixed daily schedule.

Inactive items won't be deducted — useful for backup stock.

Step 04
Reading the Stock Level Indicators

Each category shows remaining days and a color indicator:

  • 🟢 Green: Stock sufficient (14+ days)
  • 🔴 Red: Stock low (under 14 days) — reorder needed
  • 🟠 Orange: Some sub-items running low

Step 05
Overseas Purchase & Duty-Free Tracking

The top of the home screen shows your cumulative overseas spending:

  • 🟢 Green: Within duty-free limit
  • 🔴 Red: Duty-free limit exceeded
  • 🚚 Free shipping indicator: shows if threshold is met

Automatically switches between H1 / H2 each half-year.

Duty-free rules can be changed in Settings (TW / JP / KR).

Step 06
Import Products via URL

Go to the "Log / Import" tab and paste a product URL.

The system will automatically parse the product name, dosage, and quantity.

Supported platforms:

  • ✅ iHerb (full support)
  • ✅ Amazon (partial support)
  • 🔶 Shopee / Coupang / momo / Costco

You can manually edit any field if parsing is incomplete.

[IMG-06-en]

Step 09
AI Analysis Feature

The "Analysis" tab generates AI consultation prompts:

  • ① Enter your age, gender, and health goals
  • ② Select supplements to include
  • ③ Choose analysis questions
  • ④ Tap "Generate Analysis"
  • ⑤ Copy and paste into ChatGPT or Claude

Your current inventory is automatically included in the prompt.

AI Analysis only generates a prompt — no data is sent to any server.

Step 11
Quick Reorder

After logging a purchase, uHerbSync remembers your source platform. You can reorder from three entry points:

Entry 1 — Tap category on home screen
  • Opens your default platform set in Settings
  • Best for: manual entries or platform switching
Entry 2 — Tap sub-item on home screen
  • Auto-detects original purchase platform
  • Returns directly to original product page
  • Best for: fixed-platform repeat purchases
Entry 3 — Tap history record
  • Find past purchases in History tab
  • One tap returns to original store
  • Best for: finding a specific past item
