Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • Anonymous identifier (UUID)
  • App usage data (e.g., creating items, restock clicks)

2. How We Use Information

  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Analyze usage behavior
  • Enhance product features

3. Data Storage

  • Most data is stored locally on your device

This app uses third-party services such as Supabase for data storage and analytics.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data.

6. User Rights

  • You may stop using the app to stop data collection
  • You may delete the app to remove local data

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data.

8. Changes to This Policy
